DayJS Quiz

Answer the following questions

1. What is your graduation date in the following format: Jan 1, 1999?

2. What day of the week will 1/1/2027 be?

3. What is the current time in the following format: hours:minutes:seconds? (use a 12-hour clock)

4. What is the current Unix timestamp?

5. Parse the following Unix timestamp, 1318781876, and convert into any time/date format.

6. What is the difference in days between May 4, 2027 and today? (Hint: You can find the difference between two dates. See the documentation for Difference.)
